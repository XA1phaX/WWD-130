About Us

How we got here

We started in 2019, in the small college town of Rexburg Idaho. A young freshman student of Brigham Young University Idaho came up with an idea. That idea was to make t-shirts that not only looked good, but had designs that helped express peoples love for the outdoors. When coming up with a name for this new company, he thought long and hard to find a name with would encourage others to step out of the social norms and encourage others to be adventurous. One day, while still pondering the name, he overheard a stranger saying that others should live a more "tame" life. At that moment, he realized that he fundamentaly disagreed with the stranger. He wanted to encourage people to try new things, and rediscover the world outside of the typical 9-5, even if it is outside of the social norms. He wanted to encourage people to live their lives UNATMED. With that realization, Untamed Shirts was born.

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage others to discover the world, outside of the cubical and mundane routines of life by creating t-shirts that express the joy of adventure, the amazing things that the ourdoors can offer, and to inspire others to live life UNTAMED. We want to make sure that people are not only able to experience the world, but have a shirt that will keep up with all the new adventures they are destined to find.

About the Founder

Born and raised in Southern California, Jackson Clarke, known by his friends as "JT", always is searching for his next adventure. He never felt like he "fit in" with the others around him. While others were focused on doing what society told them to do, he was always looking for something new, never satisfied with the thought of sacrificing his love for adventure for the approval of others. He wanted to show others that there is more to life than just doing what society expects of them. This desire led him down the path to create Untamed Shirts. He is currently still a student at BYU-Idaho, and is looking forward to the opportunity to continue his adventure of insiring others to live life UNTAMED.
