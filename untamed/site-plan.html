Overview

Purpose

Outdoor enthusiasts enjoy talking about their adventures, but sometimes it's hard to bring up the topic with others who do not share our love for escaping civilization. I make shirts that express our love for the uncommon and are great conversation starters for our experiences in the outdoors. My websiteâ€™s purpose is to show others the adventure shirts that I make so that they can express their love for the wilderness with others and have a really cool shirt that will stand up to any of the adventures they choose to take it on.

Audience

My intended audience for this website is people who are interested in the outdoors. The age range of people I intend my website to be for is 16-40. My website will mainly be for displaying the shirts and hats that I make in order to allow my users to find out more about my shirt company. I intend the shirts to be for outdoorsy people and people interested in a more adventurous style of shirts and hats.

Content

Home page

My website is a place to display the products I sell, which include shirts and hats. People will come to my site to learn more about my company and to view and purchase my products. The pages I will have on my website are, Home, Products, and an About Us page. The header of the website will include links to these three pages, as well as have the Untamed logo in the top left. The footer will also have links to all the pages, as well as legal information, such as copyright and such. The footer will also include a link to our Instagram page, as well as a Facebook link.

The homepage will consist of a large banner image with the company name or logo on top. The page will have a few images of models wearing our shirts on the left and right of the page, as well as a little information about the company, and links to our 2 currently available products. It will also include any updates or information concerning future products. The majority of the page will be images of our products being worn by models in either a gallery or set up in a grid. There will also be a section that has a link to our Instagram page, as well as information about how to order products until we set up a way to buy them on our website.

Images for the Home page

Products

On the products page, I will have pictures of all available products, as well as the current prices. I would also like to display the available sizes and colors in this section. Currently, I have 1 shirt available and 1 hat. The shirt I have available is called “Iceberg” and has a large print of an iceberg on the back, as well as est. 2019 which is when I started my company. On the front of the shirt, it has the Untamed logo. Currently, the shirt is available in short and long sleeves, and the only available color at the moment is black. The hat I currently have available is a black snapback, with the untamed logo embroidered on the front left side.

Users will also be able to see products that are not released yet and sold out products on this page. This page will have product images displayed for each product, as well as the product price and name below the image in a grid format. Each product will say whether it is currently available or sold out. Products that are made to order will have a message stating that it is not currently available, but orders are still accepted.

Images for the Products Page

About Us

Our about us will have information about how we started as a company. The page will consist of a section about how our company came to be, our established year, the reason we started the company, a picture of me, the owner and founder of the company, and a way to contact me directly via email for questions and concerns regarding the company.

At the top of the page, I will have the section about how our company started, followed by the section about why we started the company. Following those two paragraphs, I will have a picture of myself on the right-hand side of the page with a header that says “About the Founder”, followed by some information about me and how I started the company as a student at Brigham Young University Idaho. Following that, I will have a section that is titled “Contact Us” which will have a link to our company Instagram page, my personal Instagram page, as well as my email address.
